Features

Built for riders

Every feature designed around the way you actually ride.

🏍

Auto Ride Detection

Automatically detects when you start and stop riding — no need to open the app. Works even when your phone is in your pocket.

📊

Live Telemetry

Speed, lean angle, G-force, and route data recorded in real-time. Review detailed charts and stats after every ride.

👥

Group Rides

Create or join group rides with friends. See everyone's position on the map, compare telemetry, and scrub through the ride together.

🗺

Route Mapping

Every ride mapped with scenic and twisty scores. Discover your best roads and share routes with the community.

⚡

Event Detection

Detects hard braking, rapid acceleration, and other events. See them on the map with detailed data like G-force and speed.

🔒

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device by default. Cloud sync is optional and you control what gets shared with group ride members.
